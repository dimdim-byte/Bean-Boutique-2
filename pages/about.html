About Us!

At Bean Boutique, we craft flavorful, high-quality coffee brews designed to awaken the senses. Just one sip can spark energy and joy that lasts the whole day.

Our journey began in 2020, when our founder Myint Mo discovered her love for coffee during a quiet home stay. What started as a small passion project soon evolved into a pursuit of perfection. Her dedication never faded — and it’s the same spirit that defines us today.

Our signature brew remained a hidden treasure for years, refined through patience and care. Now, we’re proud to share it with the world — one cup at a time.

We also offer subscription plans, delivering freshly roasted beans and carefully curated blends straight to your doorstep. Every batch is handcrafted with precision and heart, ensuring each cup feels personal and full of warmth.

At Bean Boutique, coffee is more than a beverage — it’s a daily ritual, a moment of comfort, and a bridge between people.

Our mission is simple: to craft coffee that inspires connection — one sip, one story, one moment at a time.
